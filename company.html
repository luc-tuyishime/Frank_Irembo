What we do

We create beautiful digital experiences that make a difference

On July 20th 2015, Irembo was built to improve Rwanda’s service delivery under a 25 years agreement with the government of Rwanda with the mandate to Build, Operate and Transfer a one stop platform for all government services. For the past three years, 89 services requested by over 8000 citizens and businesses daily were put on the platform. This evolution pragmatically transformed the country’s service delivery by making it simpler, faster and less costly.

By enabling the government to provide services to the citizens and businesses, service delivery is now processed with ease and efficiency through an online Portal, Mobile USSD and the best of breed Agent Network that were developed to support this change allowing users with or without access to internet or mobile phones to request and receive government services.
