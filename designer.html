Director of People and Culture

Full-time

Irembo is a technology startup company that is looking to revolutionize digital service delivery and make products citizens love. Irembo has three years of experience creating the online platform irembo.gov and we are using that experience to develop a wide range of products that will transform service delivery as we know it.

Our Director of People & Culture will lead the charge in building an outstanding work environment that allows our team to thrive and do extraordinary work - from planning fun company events, to implementing new hiring strategies, designing the best employee benefits package to making sure the office is running as efficiently as possible

What you will do

  • Collaborate as a member of the leadership team to map talent strategy to business strategy. Strategic vision — ability to see where the business is headed and develop programs proactively to make sure our team is able to deliver.
  • Managing our hiring process and ensuring that Irembo is an employer of choice in our field.
  • Promote a progressive culture, champion and grow Irembo’s company culture, helping the team continually live our values through effective programs and internal communications to promote morale and achieve our goals.
  • Enhance our talent-management process including recruiting, on-boarding, performance management, team development, retention and succession.
  • Create progressive and proactive compensation and benefits programs to motivate, incentivize and reward performance. Partner with the leadership team and lead the implementation of high-performance systems, including OKRs, 360 degree reviews.
  • Be a thought leader for managers on organizational and people related matters.
  • Lead the development of an office and physical environment that supports and drives performance, collaboration and supports the company’s values

You will be a good fit if

  • You have a successful track record of building and delivering talent acquisition and people programs from the ground up in a fast-growing organization.
  • You are a people person, easy to talk to with excellent written and verbal communication skills.
  • You have a genuine desire to help people grow.
  • Strong understanding of progressive company cultures and knowledge of industry best practices.
  • You are open minded; can listen and are assertive; can firmly share your perspective.
  • You have a keen eye for spotting inefficiencies. We love to improve ourselves and our processes and we know there are many that can be 100x better!
  • You are highly organized and can drive routine processes ( e.g employee appraisals) and new tasks (e.g revision of benefits packages) with ease and quality.
  • You are versatile and have diverse perspectives to advise other department leads on better management skills and create workshops to teach various human management principles.
